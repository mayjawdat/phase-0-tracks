Elevator Robot

For each passenger that enters elevator:
- Ask "What floor?"
- Listen for answer
- Convert answer to integer
- Review elevator buttons until integer = number on button
- Push button



Feeding my Cat

Every morning:
- Check contents of food bowl
- If food bowl is empty:
  - Pick up food cup
  - Until food cup contains 3/4 c dry food:
    - Scoop dry food using cup
  - Empty contents of cup into food bowl
  - Replace food cup
- Otherwise:
  - Move on to next step

- Pick up water bowl
  - Empty contents of water bowl into sink
  - Turn on faucet
  - Fill bowl with water until bowl is 3/4 full
  - Turn off faucet
  - Place bowl on floor next to food dish
